DIRECTIONAL DECISIONS
----------------------

1. MODULE NAVIGATION: Sidebar organized by groups (Main, People, Business,
   Finance, System). Each group has a label and collapsible module list.

2. STAFF ACCESS CONTROL (Prompt 46 — final approach): Per-module granular
   access on each staff member, shown as round icons with dropdown. Replaced
   the earlier "Role Groups" approach (Prompt 44) which used predefined
   group templates (Admin=all, Operations=People+Business+Finance, etc.).

3. COMPANY SETTINGS LAYOUT (Prompt 46 — final approach): Horizontal tabs
   with sidebar navigation (Option C). Replaced collapsible cards (Option A)
   and redesigned inline layout (Option B).

4. PAIRINGS vs ASSIGNMENTS: Renamed from "Assignments" to "Pairings" to
   avoid confusion with assigning courses to mentees.

5. COURSE BILLING MODES: Two modes — one-time (fixed price, optional due
   date) and monthly recurring (charged until mentee completes all lessons).
   Both support an optional one-time setup fee.

6. ENGAGEMENT CANCELLATION POLICY: Configurable per-engagement or fall back
   to org default. Defines cancel window, and credit outcomes for: cancel
   within window, cancel outside window, and no-show.

7. MENTEE FLOW: An ordered sequence of statuses, courses, and engagements
   that define a mentee's progression through the organization. Statuses
   can be toggled in/out of the flow. Mentees track their current position.

8. PAY TYPES: Four types (Hourly, Salary, % Monthly Profit, % Engagement
   Profit). Org admins configure which types are available per role in
   Company Settings. Individual staff get assigned one type + rate.

9. ASSISTANT MENTOR: Added as a distinct role (admin, mentor,
   assistant_mentor, staff). Gets its own People module sidebar entry
   and role-specific pay type options.

10. PUSH TO MAIN: From Prompt 15 onward, all changes were pushed directly
    to main (user's explicit instruction for the session).
