Features

One platform for your entire operation

Stop juggling tools. Manage mentors, mentees, meetings, courses, invoices, and payroll from a single dashboard.

Mentor and Mentee Management

Full profiles with contact info, avatars, messaging preferences, and custom notes. Assign mentees to mentors, track enrollment status, and manage the entire relationship.

Meeting Scheduling with Credits

Define meetings per billing cycle, track credits automatically, and set cancellation policies. Credits can roll over or expire based on your rules.

Course Builder

Create structured courses with lessons, embedded video, quizzes, and short-answer questions. Control delivery with scheduled release or sequential unlock.

Invoicing and Billing

Create individual or batch invoices, track payment status, set custom payment terms, and manage overdue detection automatically.

Mentor Payroll

Four compensation models: percentage of revenue, flat monthly, per-meeting, or hourly. Each mentor can have their own pay structure.

Staff Permissions

Granular role-based access control. Toggle permissions per module so each staff member sees exactly what they need and nothing more.
